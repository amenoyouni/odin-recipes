Karaage

Ingredients

  • Chiken Breast
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Flour

How to make it

  1. Cut chicken into small pieces
  2. Soak chicken in soy sauce, garlic,ginger mixture for about 30 mins to an hour
  3. After soaking the chicken, bread with flour
  4. Heat oil up in a pan to about 350f
  5. Put chicken carefuly into oil and cook for about 5-7 mins
