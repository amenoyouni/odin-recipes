Udon

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodles
  • Beef (Thinly sliced)
  • Hon Dashi Fish Stock
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce

How to make it

  1. Sear the beef on a pan
  2. Add soy sauce and sugar to the beef
  3. Once cooked add water and dashi fish stock
  4. When the water starts to boil, add the noodles
  5. Garnish with pepper flakes and green onion
